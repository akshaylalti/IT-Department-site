HOD, Lecturer

Information is the life-blood of any organization, it needs to be relevant, accurate and available promptly. The shortage of professionals equipped with a sound knowledge and skills who can provide prompt and accurate IT solutions, led to a formal UG level course in Information Technology. The aim of the department is to become a centre of excellence in the field of IT & related research and to churn out professional to become leaders in innovation, entrepreneurship, and creativity in the IT field.
